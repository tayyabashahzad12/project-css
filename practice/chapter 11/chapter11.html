The Composition of Seawater

Alomost everything can be find in seawater.This include dissolved materials from earth crusl as well as materials released from organism.The most important componenets of seawater that influence life forms are salinity ,temprature,dissolved gasses(mostly oxygen and carbon dioxide),nutreiets,and pH.These elements vary in composition as well as their influence on marine life.
